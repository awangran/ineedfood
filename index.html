39.5 Million people


in the United States alone live in food deserts. Some may not even realize it. Other people might have extra food they don’t know how to use, or wish to help those who are less fortunate. Food Oasis gives everyone a place to start.

Born out of the desire to help those who struggle to find quality food, Food Oasis' mission is to empower people living within food deserts to learn about options available to them. We also aim to provide volunteer and donation opportunities in order to help levy the burden faced by those living in food deserts.
